About StudyVibes

StudyVibes is a free pomodoro timer paired with curated lofi music, made for students, researchers, and remote workers who want to focus without friction. No sign-up, no clutter — pick a vibe, start the timer, and get into flow.

How it works

The timer follows the Pomodoro Technique: 25-minute focus sessions separated by short breaks, with a longer break after four sessions. As you work, a lofi stream plays in the background — rainy lofi, sleep & chill, coffee jazz, nature lo-fi, or bossa nova — each with its own atmosphere and animated backdrop.

Why we built it

Most focus tools are either bloated or hide their best features behind a paywall. StudyVibes keeps the core experience free forever. An optional Premium plan removes ads, unlocks a custom timer, and helps support the project.

How StudyVibes stays free

  • Ads — we show a limited number of ads through Google AdSense.
  • Gear we love — product links in the sidebars are Amazon affiliate links; if you buy through them we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.
  • Premium — a small monthly or lifetime plan for an ad-free experience.
